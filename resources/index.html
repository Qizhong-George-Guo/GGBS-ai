How These Resources Connect to GGBS

The Resources section is intended to serve both as a personal archive and as a practical library for collaborators, students, and practitioners who want to apply the GGBS Framework.

  • Publications provide the scientific and conceptual foundations for GGBS.
  • Technical reports and project documents show how concepts are applied in real-world settings.
  • Teaching materials help train the next generation of engineers and planners.
  • Tools and data support hands-on exploration and analysis.
  • Media and outreach materials help explain GGBS ideas to broader audiences.

Many resources will naturally link back to specific Applications and Framework pages, reinforcing the structure of the site.
